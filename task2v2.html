The exterior of the G-Class.

Discover the highlights of the exterior design of the G-Class with three different equipment lines - PROFESSIONAL Line Exterior, EXCLUSIVE Line Exterior and AMG Line. Find out more about the other highlights such as the robust front, the exposed spare wheel on the tailgate or the striking wheel arch linings.



Scope for your individual equipment.

With the Night package, your G-Class looks even more expressive. Depending on the selected paint finish, highlights in obsidian black or night black magno characterise the exterior. This creates contrasts or smooth transitions. With the optional Black Accents, scopes of appointments in obsidian black or night black magno enable you to complete the individual appearance. Components:

  • Darkened indicators and tail lights as well as headlamps
  • Exterior protective strip with trim insert in a black pinstripe look (C90), only in conj. with EXCLUSIVE Line Exterior (P23) and AMG Line (P31)
  • Underguard in black (C49)
  • 45.7 cm (18-inch) 5-spoke light-alloy wheels painted in matt black (RK7), in conj. with PROFESSIONAL Line Exterior (P03)
  • 48.3 cm (19-inch) 5-twin-spoke light-alloy wheels (RJ4) painted in black, in conj. with EXCLUSIVE Line Exterior (P23)
  • 50.8 cm (20-inch) AMG multi-spoke light-alloy wheels painted in high-gloss black (RJ5), in conj. with AMG Line (P31)

Depending on the choice of paint finish, the following scopes are finished in obsidian black (for most metallic paints) or night black magno (for all magno paints):

  • Radiator grille including louvres and surround of Mercedes star
  • Outside mirror housings
  • Spare wheel ring, only in conj. with EXCLUSIVE Line Exterior (P23) and AMG Line (P31)
  • Insert in bumper, only in conj. with AMG Line (P31)
